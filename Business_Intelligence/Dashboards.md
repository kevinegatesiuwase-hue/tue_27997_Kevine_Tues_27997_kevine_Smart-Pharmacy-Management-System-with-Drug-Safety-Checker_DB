# Dashboards – Smart Pharmacy Management System

## 1. Overview

Dashboards provide visual insights into key pharmacy metrics and trends for management, pharmacists, and regulatory oversight.  
They help monitor medication safety, inventory status, dispensing performance, and compliance in real time.

---

## 2. Recommended Dashboards

### 2.1 Executive Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of pharmacy performance:

- Total medications in inventory
- Total prescriptions processed
- Total medications dispensed
- Number of near-expiry and expired batches
- Low-stock medication count

---

### 2.2 Inventory Management Dashboard

Focuses on stock control and expiry monitoring:

- Current stock levels by medication
- Batch-wise inventory status
- Near-expiry and expired batches
- FIFO compliance indicators
- Stock alerts and replenishment needs

---

### 2.3 Dispensing & Operations Dashboard

Monitors daily pharmacy operations:

- Daily/weekly dispensing volumes
- Dispensed vs pending prescriptions
- Dispensing activity by pharmacist
- Quantity prescribed vs quantity dispensed
- Override actions during dispensing

---

### 2.4 Drug Safety & Alerts Dashboard

Ensures patient safety and clinical compliance:

- Drug–drug interaction alerts triggered
- Patient allergy warnings
- Interaction severity distribution
- Medications most frequently involved in alerts
- Override frequency and reasons

---

### 2.5 Audit & Compliance Dashboard

Supports regulatory reporting and inspections:

- Recent audit log activities
- Inventory changes over time
- Dispensing overrides with justification
- User activity (who performed which action)
- Compliance status indicators

---

## 3. Dashboard Features

- Filter by date range
- Drill-down into individual medications, batches, prescriptions, or patients
- Role-based access control
- Export dashboards and data to PDF, Excel, or CSV
- Real-time or near real-time data refresh
